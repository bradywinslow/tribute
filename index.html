Lebron James

One of the greatest basketball players of all time

Career Achievements and Awards

  • 4x NBA champion (2012, 2013, 2016, 2020)
  • 4x NBA Finals MVP (2012, 2013, 2016, 2020)
  • 4x NBA Most Valuable Player (2009, 2010, 2012, 2013)
  • 19x NBA All-Star (2005-2023)
  • 3x NBA All-Star Game MVP (2006, 2008, 2018)
  • 13x All-NBA First Team (2006, 2008-2018, 2020)
  • 3x All-NBA Second Team (2005, 2007, 2021)
  • 2x All-NBA Third Team (2019, 2022)
  • 5x NBA All-Defensive First Team (2009-2013)
  • NBA All-Defensive Second Team (2014)
  • NBA Rookie of the Year (2004)
  • NBA All-Rookie First Team (2004)
  • NBA Scoring Leader (2008)
  • NBA Assists Leader (2020)
  • NBA 75th Anniversary Team
  • NBA All-Time Leading Scorer
  • 4th Most Assists in NBA history
  • 9th Most Steals in NBA history

He is the greatest physical presence to ever play the game, its most dominant all-around talent. He is in one body the combination of every great player who ever lived: the size and power to dominate his position as Chamberlain and Shaq dominated theirs, the court vision and team-play disposition of Bird and Magic, the speed, athleticism and vertical game of Jordan, Dominique or Kobe.

--Howard Bryant
